Akademische Komplizenschaft beenden!

As students and staff of Leipzig University, we take the University's declaration of commitment to the dignity of all people seriously. The report presented on this page details Leipzig University's direct and indirect support of Israel in its violations of international law through cooperations with Israeli universities, which are embedded in the Israeli political system and in many cases directly contribute to the illegal occupation of Palestine and the genocidal war in Gaza. We call on Leipzig University to honour its ethical commitment to human rights by suspending its cooperations with Israeli universities and academic institutions.

This is a non violent and anti racist campaign. We advocate for an institutional boycott of these institutions on the basis of their complicity in apartheid and genocide; we explicitly reject any discrimination against individual people on the basis of their race, nationality, religion, or any other characteristic of their identity.
